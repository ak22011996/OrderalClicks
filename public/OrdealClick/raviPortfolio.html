About Me:

Photography is not only about capturing the images but also about freezing the moment for a lifetime. Talking about myself, I am an aspiring photographer who is always on his toes to borrow some moments from one’s life and freeze it in the form of a photograph. For this, I try to capture natural and elegant portraits that could open doors to memories from the past. In addition to the portraits and candid pictures, I love to capture events and corporate outings as well.In case, you are concerned about my work ethics and professionalism, then I would ask you to give me a chance rather than boasting about myself. And when it comes to tools that I use for photography, it is my eyes through which I lock the beauty forever.Bangalore is the current location where I am trying to capture the emotions and beauty in its every form. So, please come and watch your emotions turning into images to be cherished forever.
